This defines the structure of the GPS beacon.

The GPS beacon consists of eight members: the type of beacon (type), the decimal identifier (deci), the latitude (latitude), longitude (longitude), speed (speed), angle (angle), altitude (altitude), and number of satellites in use (satellites).A diagram of the struct is included below.
