Detailed Description

The magnetometer beacon structure.

This defines the structure of the magnetometer beacon.

The magnetometer beacon consists of five members: the type of beacon (type), the decimal identifier (deci), and the magnetometer values for the x, y, and z axes (magx, magy, magz). A diagram of the struct is included below.

+--------+----------+----------+----------+----------+
|  type  |   deci   |   magx   |   magy   |   magz   |
+--------+----------+----------+----------+----------+
