int32_t Artemis::Teensy::PDU::get_switch ( PDU_SW  sw)

Gets the status of a switch on the PDU.

The status packet is sent to the PDU, and a response is awaited. If, after 5 cycles of 5 seconds, there is no response, the attempt fails. The response is (optionally) printed to the serial console.
