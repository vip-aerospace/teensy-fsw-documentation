Detailed Description

The PDU packet structure.

This defines the structure of a PDU packet.

The PDU packet consists of three members: the type of packet (type), the switch to be controlled (sw), and the state of that switch (sw_state).A diagram of the struct is included below.

+--------+--------+-----------+
|  type  |   sw   |  sw_state |
+--------+--------+-----------+

State is represented as a number. Values greater than zero are on, and zero or less is off. By default, the switch state is off.
